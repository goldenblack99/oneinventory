Container Pickup Process Flow


Truck Arrival

The prime mover enters Depot 02.

Obtain an IN Ticket from the Guard Room.

Container Pickup

Proceed to the counter and hand over the required documents.

The forklift operator retrieves the container from the yard..

Loading & Locking

The container is loaded onto the trailer and secured properly.

Gate Out

Present the loaded container to the Yard Supervisor for inspection.

Proceed to the counter and obtain the Gate Pass.

Hand over the duplicate copy of the Gate Pass to the Guard Room.

Exit the depot.
